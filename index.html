INVASION DAY

  • < Prev
  • Next >
  • >|

I arrived on a Monday morning.
I had no visa. No passport stamp.
No immigration official met me.
I just disembarked the plane.

There was no welcome.
No give, no take.
I did not sing the songs of my ancestors and I did not listen to a single word sung.
There was nobody to listen to. Nobody to take permission from.

I obeyed the instructions of as many ants.
I filled out their forms and fed their machines.
I signed my name on the dotted line.
I joined the ranks of the prevailing culture, that is no culture at all.
It's people have no land to call their own.

As it stands I am another occupier.
A guest of the intruders.
An ally of the invaders.

Sovereignty was never ceded.
